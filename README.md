## FAQ

**Is Phoenix Executor safe to use?**  
You are responsible for how you use it and for protecting your account. The executor does not gather personal information, but third-party tools with Roblox always involve some risk. Make sure you understand the license and terms before using it.

**Will it work after Roblox updates?**  
Phoenix Executor has automatic update support designed to help it stay compatible with newer Roblox versions. Even so, immediate support after a major patch is not guaranteed.

**How does this compare to other executors?**  
Phoenix Executor puts more emphasis on a light footprint, an integrated script hub, and multilingual access. Different executors offer different capabilities; this one is aimed at dependable use and simple operation instead of advanced bypass methods.

**Can my Roblox account be banned?**  
Any script executor use goes against Roblox's Terms of Service. Bans or other account actions can happen. Use it at your own risk and consider a separate account for testing.

**Where are scripts stored locally?**  
Your scripts are kept in the `scripts/user/` directory inside the installation folder. Hub content is also cached locally so it can be accessed offline.
